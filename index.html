About Us

We are Team Introspectors. We design and develop applications that aim to solve real world problems. The current problem we are addressing is Climate change and waste management. Worldwide consumption and production drives the global economy, yet the current use of natural resources is unsustainable. The global material footprint rose nearly 18 percent from 73 billion metric tons in 2010 to 85.9 billion metric tons in 2017, the UN reports. Hence with a thought of creating an impact and reducing waste production, we have developed Recycle it!, a marketplace for producers and consumers to build and buy products in a sustainable way for our society.
